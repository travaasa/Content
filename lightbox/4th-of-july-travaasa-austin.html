Red, White and Blue 4th of July Menu

Join us on June 30th and July 4th at Travaasa Austin for food, wine, and fun to celebrate America’s day of independence.  Enjoy a red, white and blue themed menu created by Travaasa Austin’s Executive Chef Ben Baker that not only tastes delicious but has a patriotic feel to it.  Come for the dinner and then stay for the night.

We will also be offering our "Red, White & Blue Sangrias" throughout the week of the 4th (June 30th–July7th).  We have teamed up with Cinco Vodka® to keep things fresh, local and lively.  Our recipes will include fresh fruit, juices and herbs, champagne, Grand Marnier and, of course, Cinco Vodka distilled from 100% American Wheat and made right here in central Texas.


The Beginning

  • Blue corn crusted soft shell blue crab, Buena Tierra watermelon-rosie basil relish, red hot Chile de Arbol remoulade, Bella Verdi Farms butter leaf lettuce
  • Radicchio and cherry salad, Texas grapefruit citronette, shaved Parmesan Reggiano
  • McCall Creek Farms Ruby heirloom tomato tart, baby lettuces, Texas goat cheese

The Middle

  • Gulf caught red drum veronique, red grape-chocolate mint buerre blanc
  • Texas raised Black Angus tenderloin, crawfish sauce
  • Stuffed red bell with achiote quinoa, and grilled tomatillo-sweet corn relish

The End

  • Home-spun blueberry sherbet
  • Fresh strawberry pie
  • Texas cheese cake with fresh raspberry compote
