Travaasa Tax Relief

To celebrate the end of tax season, Travaasa is offering a month long series of special classes, rates and programming designed to help guests relax, recharge and hopefully, spend some of their hard earned tax return! Available at both Travaasa Austin and Travaasa Hana throughout the month of April, guests who stay two or more nights have the chance to enhance their stay by selecting from a menu of $15 options, including:

  • Extend any 50-minute spa massage to an 80-minute massage
  • Half-hour personal training
  • One-on-one yoga and nutrition session
  • An array of hand-selected bottles of wine

Retail value of these items is $35 - $65 dollars.

At Travaasa Hana, guests can splurge on a delicious 15 course tasting menu including wine pairings for just $150 per person.

*Travaasa's Tax Relief menu of $15 experiences are subject to availability and all experiences may not be offered at each property. Spa treatments and personal training must be booked in advance. Signature musical massages not included.
