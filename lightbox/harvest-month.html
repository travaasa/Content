Culture

GHOST STORIES – Austin & Hana, Maui
Spirits abound during the month of Halloween. Join us around the Travaasa campfire to hear about our local ghosts in residence as well as share other ghostly tales from the beyond.

ALOHA WEEK – Hana, Maui
Aloha Week is Hawai'i’s premier cultural showcase, a celebration of our music, dance and history intended to preserve the unique island traditions. Please note that the opening day (October 13th) of this event will hold many people in Hāna and a portion of the road will be closed from 11am to 1pm. Families from all of Maui drive to Hāna for this specific day as it brings to pass the reenactment of our ali’i (royal families). There will be Lively Entertainment and we want to invite you to explore and find out what makes Aloha Festivals special.
